About

Artisans are the backbone of India’s non-farm rural economy, engaged in craft production to earn a livelihood. However, propelled by loss of markets, declining skills and difficulty catering to new markets, the number of Indian artisans has been rapidly decreasing. Crafting a Livelihood calls for re-investment in India’s artisans to safeguard history, culture and an important source of livelihood.
With big international platforms like Amazon and Facebook looking to invest in small businesses and support local artisans, a social website to share and support them is the need of the hour.
Artisans can register to the site, create a user profile, post their artwork and other users can Like and Comment on posts by clicking on the post title and entering the post details page.
Supporting these artisans has been made easier through the website with the Buy or Donate option.
